Honey-Buffalo Chicken Thighs

Ingredients

  • 2lbs Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • Honey
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Various seasonings (salt, pepper, paprika, onion/garlic powder)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into bite size pieces
  2. Toss chicken in seasonings of choice
  3. In a large skillet, cook chicken till it has reached 165 degrees internal
  4. Combine buffalo and honey in a separae bowl till you get a flavor you like
  5. Keeping the chicken in the same skillet, reduce heat to low and add sauce
  6. Cook until sauce has reduced and serve hot!

Nutritional Facts

Content Amount per Serving
Protein 50g
Sugar 30g
